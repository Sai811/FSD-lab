Colleges in Pune

College of Engineering Pune (COEP)
COEP is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, known for its heritage and excellence in education.
MIT World Peace University (MIT WPU)
MIT WPU offers a range of programs and is known for its emphasis on peace and holistic education.
Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT)
PICT is highly regarded for its focus on computer science and IT programs.
DY Patil College of Engineering
DY Patil is known for its modern campus and diverse range of engineering programs.
Fergusson College
Fergusson College is renowned for its arts and science programs, with a rich history dating back to 1885.
Symbiosis International University
Symbiosis is a leading private university offering a wide range of courses and has a global presence.
Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE)
PCCOE is a well-known institution in the Pune region, offering quality education in engineering.
