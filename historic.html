Ajinkyatara Fort

Ajinkyatara Fort is a historic fort located in the Satara district of Maharashtra. The fort is known for its rich history and scenic views from the top.

Shaniwarwada

Shaniwarwada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune. It was built in 1732 and served as the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire.

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple

This temple is one of the most revered Ganesh temples in Pune, known for its elaborate celebrations during Ganesh Chaturthi.

Lavasa

Lavasa is a planned hill city located near Pune. It's known for its picturesque landscapes and modern infrastructure.

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

The Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum showcases a vast collection of artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and everyday objects from Maharashtra's history.
