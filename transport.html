Transport in Pune

Pune offers a comprehensive bus network operated by PMPML, connecting various parts of the city efficiently.

The Pune Metro provides a rapid transit option, significantly reducing travel time across the city.

Auto rickshaws are a popular mode of transport in Pune, offering flexible and affordable travel options.
Taxis
